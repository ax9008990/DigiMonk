About Us

Preserving the Spiritual Heritage of Sikkim through Digitalization

Our Mission

Our mission is to digitally preserve and showcase the rich cultural, spiritual, and architectural heritage of the monasteries in Sikkim. By integrating technology with tradition, we aim to create a platform where history meets the digital age, enabling access for researchers, travelers, and future generations.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Sikkim’s monasteries are digitally preserved, accessible globally, and appreciated by all. Our vision is to bridge the gap between ancient traditions and modern technology, creating immersive experiences that allow everyone to explore the beauty and wisdom of Sikkim’s monasteries without boundaries.

Core Principles

• Authentic Preservation: Keeping the original essence alive.
• Accessibility: Making monasteries’ culture available worldwide.
• Innovation: Blending digital tools with cultural heritage.
• Education: Providing resources for learners, researchers, and explorers.

Join Us

Be a part of this journey to preserve the soul of Sikkim’s monasteries. Together, we can ensure that these timeless treasures remain alive for future generations in the digital world.
